Yourself as a Brand

Your identity is your asset. Work on China-facing business in Japan, and Japan-facing business in China.

I do two things: translate inward—bringing foreign brands to Chinese consumers; translate outward—bringing Chinese brands to the Japanese market. Not a cultural broker, but a context decoder. What I enjoy most is catching subtle differences—in design details, brand tones, and market contexts. When that ability is seen, that's my best work.
